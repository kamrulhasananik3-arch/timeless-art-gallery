About the Gallery

Timeless Art Gallery is a digital celebration of art across centuries — from Renaissance masterpieces to modern abstract expression.

We aim to make art accessible, educational, and inspiring for everyone while providing a space for creators to share their work.

Our Values

  • Accessibility — Free, high-quality viewing for all
  • Education — Context and stories behind every artwork
  • Community — Welcoming original creations from artists
  • Respect — Honoring creators and cultural heritage

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of art can I upload?

Original artwork, digital paintings, traditional scans, photography — anything you created or have rights to share.

Is uploading free?

Yes — completely free with no hidden costs.

How are uploads reviewed?

All submissions are checked for appropriateness and originality (usually within 24–48 hours).

What coding languages are used to build this site?

This gallery is built with 3 coding languages: HTML (page structure), CSS (styling & layout), and JavaScript (interactive features like image modals and upload previews). No frameworks or build tools are required.
